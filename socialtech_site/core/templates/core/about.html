Our Story

Founded in 2020, SocialTech Innovations was born from a simple observation: nonprofits and social enterprises doing the world's most important work often lack access to the technology resources they need to maximize their impact.

We saw incredible organizations struggling with outdated systems, paying full price for software they could barely afford, and lacking the technical expertise to modernize their operations. Meanwhile, technology companies had resources to donate, and skilled tech professionals wanted to contribute to social causes.

SocialTech Innovations was created to bridge this gap. We partner with leading technology providers to secure donated and discounted products, while connecting mission-driven organizations with affordable IT services, custom development, and capacity-building programs. Today, we're proud to serve over 500 organizations across 25 countries, helping them leverage technology to transform lives and communities.

Our Mission

To democratize access to technology for nonprofits, NGOs, and social enterprises, enabling them to operate more efficiently, reach more beneficiaries, and create lasting social impact through innovative and affordable IT solutions.

Our Vision

A world where every social impact organization, regardless of size or budget, has access to the technology tools and expertise needed to achieve their mission and create meaningful change in their communities.
